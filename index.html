How to play

Watch the short video or follow the steps.

Steps

  1. Press S to start.
  2. Hold Space to grow the stick.
  3. Release Space to drop the stick.
  4. If the stick perfectly reaches the next platform—your hero crosses safely.
  5. Miss the distance (too short/too long) and it’s game over.
Pro tip: try to land near the center of the next platform for scoring double.
